üöó Day Trip Ideas

From Sofia:
  • Seven Rila Lakes ‚Äì Stunning glacier lakes at 2,100-2,500m elevation. Main hiking route is 2.5-3 hours to the highest lake. Can book organized tour from Sofia or drive to Rila Mountains.
  • Rila Monastery ‚Äì One of Bulgaria's most important cultural monuments, dating to 10th century. Incredible frescos, icons, and wood carvings. 2 hours from Sofia, shared shuttles run daily.
From Plovdiv:
  • Starosel Winery ‚Äì Drink wine underground in a room that looks like a Thracian temple! Can visit for the day even without staying overnight. Restaurant and winery open during the day.
From Bucharest:
  • Pele»ô Castle ‚Äì Stunning Neo-Renaissance castle in the Carpathians. Consider a full day trip or even overnight stay? (Train accessible)
From Bra»ôov:
  • Bran Castle ‚Äì "Dracula's Castle" ‚Äì nearby and easy day trip

üèõÔ∏è City Highlights

Sofia:
  • Vitosha Boulevard ‚Äì Main pedestrian street with shops, cafes, views of Vitosha Mountain. Great for walking, window shopping, stopping for a beer.
  • Alexander Nevsky Cathedral ‚Äì Iconic gold-domed cathedral
  • Sofia Opera and Ballet ‚Äì Surprisingly affordable (~$8 tickets!). Dress up a bit for fun. Get there early for cheap sparkling wine at the bar (~$3/glass).

  • Tsarevets Fortress ‚Äì Medieval fortress on a hill with views over the city. Former capital of Second Bulgarian Kingdom (12th-14th century).
  • Street Art Scene ‚Äì Look for the painted staircases!
  • Shtastliveca Restaurant ‚Äì Jen's favorite Bulgarian restaurant! Now a small chain throughout Bulgaria.
  • Handmade Crafts ‚Äì Big artisan movement here, authentic Bulgarian souvenirs vs generic tourist stuff.

üçΩÔ∏è Bulgarian Food to Try

Banitsa ‚Äì Flaky buttery pastry with white cheese (like feta). Sometimes with bacon or spinach.
Shopska Salad ‚Äì The national salad: red peppers, cucumbers, onion, parsley, shaved white cheese.
Mekitsa ‚Äì Warm fried dough with jam, honey, yogurt, or powdered sugar. Like Hungarian langos.
Kebapche ‚Äì Grilled pork or beef sausages, served with fries or salad.
Moussaka ‚Äì Layered dish with aubergine and mincemeat.
Tarator ‚Äì Cold cucumber yogurt soup, served as a starter. An acquired taste!
Bulgarian Yogurt ‚Äì National treasure! Patented as a probiotic health supplement.
White Cheese ‚Äì Sheep's cheese similar to feta, used in everything.
